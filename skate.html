Random Skate Tricks Free

Skateboarding is an incredible artform that can provide many with a sense of peace and creativity. Some skaters might get stuck trying to think of new trick ideas. This app combines the many combinations of stances, frontside and backside, and obstacles to provide trick suggestions to keep the user thinking of fresh trick ideas! A user experience for a skater who wants new trick ideas.
