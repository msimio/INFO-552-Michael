Virtual Guitar Looper Pedal

Many recording and loop programs are filled with ads and trying to sell you a premium version. This barebones design has the traveling musician in mind and provides a simple, WYSIWYG, ad free way to work on new song ideas. Designed for a user who is constantly traveling and does not have access to proper recording equipment.
