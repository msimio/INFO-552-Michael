Quribana

Quribana allows users in the same physical space to form an unexpected connection. Rather than scrolling through your phone, you can open Quribana to find other nearby users and begin chatting. The process begins by setting up your profile with just some basic information including username, status and an emoji. You can also set your conversation topic preferences to let other users (or Qurbis) know what you would like to talk about.

If a connection is found, you will be able to view the profile before deciding to chat with the user. Once you enter into a chat, you will be able to start messaging other Qurbis for as long as your connection persists. Choose your messages wisely, as only the five most recent messages in the conversation will remain on the screen. Once the connection is lost, your messages will also be cleared and will not be collected or saved at any point in the process.

Quribana was created to help break down barriers to communication and help people connect with each other. Set up your profile, use your status to share what is on your mind and connect with other Qurbis!
